📜 Historical Background

✅ First demand for a Constituent Assembly - 1895 (Bal Gangadhar Tilak)
✅ Second demand - 1922 (Mahatma Gandhi)
✅ Third demand - 1924 (Motilal Nehru)
✅ Idea of Constituent Assembly - M. N. Roy (1934)
✅ Congress officially demanded Constituent Assembly - Faizpur Session (1937)
✅ Cabinet Mission came to India - 1946
✅ Constituent Assembly formed - 1946
